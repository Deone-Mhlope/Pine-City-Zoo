PANDAS

The giant panda (Ailuropoda melanoleuca) is a rare and beloved bear species native to the bamboo forests of China. Despite being classified as carnivores, pandas have adapted to a mostly vegetarian diet consisting almost entirely of bamboo. They can eat up to 38 kilograms of it each day! Their distinct black-and-white fur and gentle nature make them a favorite in zoos around the world. Pandas live in mountainous regions and are considered a conservation symbol due to their endangered status. In the wild, pandas live up to 20 years, but in captivity, they can live over 30 years.
